Question: Find \(\lim_{x \to 1} \frac{x^2 - 3x + 2}{x - 1}\).
Answer: -1

Question: For what value(s) of \(k\), if any, is \(f(x) = \begin{cases} x^2 - k & \text{if } x < 2 \\ k^2 - x & \text{if } x \ge 2 \end{cases}\) continuous on \((-\infty, \infty)\)?
Answer: 2

Question: If \(f(x) = \sqrt{x^2 - 8x + 1}\), then \(f'(x)\) is:
Answer: \(\frac{x - 4}{\sqrt{x^2 - 8x + 1}}\)

Question: Evaluate \(\int \frac{8}{1 + 8x} \, dx\).
Answer: \(\ln|1 + 8x| + C\)
